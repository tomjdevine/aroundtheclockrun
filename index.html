24HR RELAY

24 Hours. One Track. One Goal.

An endurance relay unlike any other: for 24 hours, teams keep one runner on the move at all times, circling the AVC track to raise critical funds for cystic fibrosis research and support through the NCFS.

April 5th & 6th 2026
24 Hours
4–6 Runners per Team
1 Goal Support the NCFS

About the Event

This 24-hour team relay run is more than just a race – it's a celebration of community, endurance, and support for those affected by Cystic Fibrosis. The Nederlandse Cystic Fibrosis Stichting (NCFS) supports research efforts to tackle this disease as well as families in their times of need.

Teams of 4-6 runners will run the 400m athletics track for 24 hours straight, a true test of endurance. While one runner from each team is on the track, the rest of the team will be supporting their runner and enjoying the campsite atmosphere in tents, party tents, or campervans next to the track, creating a festival-like feeling.

Why NCFS?

The Nederlandse Cystic Fibrosis Stichting provides crucial support to families affected by Cystic Fibrosis, including medical care, research funding, and community support. This event is our way of giving back to an organization that has made a real difference in our lives.

Event Rules & Guidelines

Running Rules

  • Only one runner per team on the track at any time
  • Teams can rotate runners as frequently as needed
  • All runners must stay within the designated track area
  • Safety is our top priority - follow all track rules

Camping & Facilities

  • Teams can set up tents, party tents, or bring campervans
  • Designated camping areas will be provided
  • Basic facilities will be available on-site
  • Respect other teams' space and keep noise levels reasonable

Fundraising

  • Each team is encouraged to raise funds for NCFS
  • Fundraising progress will be tracked throughout the event
  • All donations go directly to NCFS
  • Teams can use their own fundraising platforms
