Around the Clock Run

24-Hour Team Relay for NCFS

Join us for an epic 24-hour relay run around a 400m athletics track to raise money for the Nederlandse Cystic Fibrosis Stichting (NCFS)

24 Hours
4-6 Runners per Team

About the Event

This 24-hour team relay run is more than just a race â€“ it's a celebration of community, endurance, and support for those affected by Cystic Fibrosis. The Nederlandse Cystic Fibrosis Stichting (NCFS) has personally supported our family, making this cause deeply meaningful to us.

Teams of 4-6 runners will take turns running around a 400m athletics track for 24 hours straight. While one runner from each team is on the track, the rest of the team will be camping in tents, party tents, or campervans next to the track, creating a festival-like atmosphere.

Why NCFS?

The Nederlandse Cystic Fibrosis Stichting provides crucial support to families affected by Cystic Fibrosis, including medical care, research funding, and community support. This event is our way of giving back to an organization that has made a real difference in our lives.

Date and times are to be determined. We'll announce the final schedule soon!

Prizes

Top 3 teams for funds raised and total distance covered

Event Rules & Guidelines

Running Rules

  • Only one runner per team on the track at any time
  • Teams can rotate runners as frequently as needed
  • All runners must stay within the designated track area
  • Safety is our top priority - follow all track rules

Camping & Facilities

  • Teams can set up tents, party tents, or bring campervans
  • Designated camping areas will be provided
  • Basic facilities will be available on-site
  • Respect other teams' space and keep noise levels reasonable

Fundraising

  • Each team is encouraged to raise funds for NCFS
  • Fundraising progress will be tracked throughout the event
  • All donations go directly to NCFS
  • Teams can use their own fundraising platforms
